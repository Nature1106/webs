Resistance of a system of resistors

2021

Presentation

Resistance of a system of resistors refer to the total opposition to electric current when multiple resistors are connected in a circuit. The total resistance depends on how the resistor are connected. The total resistance determines how much current flows through the circuit for a given voltage.

Introduction to Oral Presentation

2025

Explanation

An oral presentation is a spoken form of communication where a speaker conveys information, ideas or arguments to an audience. It is commonly used in academic, professional, and public settings. The purpose is to inform, persuade, explain or entertain. It incorporate visual aids like slides or props to support the message. An effective oral presentation is well-prepared, concise, and tailored to the audience's interest and understanding.
